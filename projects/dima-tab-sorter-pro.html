Overview

Tab Sorter Pro is a cross-browser extension available on the Chrome Web Store and Microsoft Edge Add-ons, designed to transform how you manage browser tabs. Whether you're a student juggling research, a developer with dozens of documentation tabs, or just someone trying to stay organized, Tab Sorter Pro automates the chaos and lets you focus on what matters.

Key Features

Smart Auto-Sort

Intelligently organize tabs by domain, category, and purpose. One click organizes your entire workspace.

Group Management

Create custom tab groups with color coding. Work context-aware tab organization for maximum productivity.

One-Click Cleanup

Remove duplicates, close inactive tabs, and purge clutter instantly. Reclaim memory and focus.

Tab Search

Instantly find any tab across your entire browser session. No more hunting through dozens of tabs.

Keyboard Shortcuts

Power user shortcuts for instant access. Speed up your workflow with customizable hotkeys.

Cross-Device Sync

Sync your tab organization across all your devices. Start on one device, continue on another seamlessly.
